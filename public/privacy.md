# PRIVACY POLICY
**Last Updated:** December 21, 2025

**1. Introduction**

**ZedCubed LLC** ("Company", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose data when you use the GlobalPhone application ("Service"). By using the Service, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

**2. Data We Collect**

**2.1. Information You Provide**

We collect data directly from you when you interact with the Service:
* **Wallet Address:** When you connect your crypto wallet, we collect your public address to facilitate payments and authenticate your session.
* **Phone Number:** If you register as a Recipient, we collect and store your real phone number to route calls.
* **User Profile:** We collect the name and pricing preferences you set for your public profile.

**2.2. Automated & Technical Information**

When you use the Service, we automatically collect certain technical data:
* **Call Metadata:** We collect logs of calls (Call SID, duration, status, timestamps) to calculate pricing and process refunds.
* **Blockchain Data:** All payment transactions are recorded on the Base blockchain public ledger. We do not control this data.
* **Error & Performance Logs:** We use **Sentry** to track application errors and crashes. These logs may contain technical details about your device or request (e.g., IP address) to help us fix bugs.
* **Usage Analytics:** We use **Vercel Analytics** to measure website traffic and performance. This data is aggregated and anonymized.

**3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies**

We use a minimal set of cookies strictly necessary for the operation of the Service.
* **Session Tokens (JWT):** We use cookies to store your secure session token (JWT) when you authenticate. This allows you to remain logged in while using the app.
* **No Ad Tracking:** We do not use cookies for advertising, retargeting, or marketing purposes.

**4. Children's Privacy**

The Service is intended strictly for users **18 years of age or older**. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete such information immediately.

**5. How We Use Your Data**

We use the collected data for the following purposes:
* **To Connect Calls:** Your phone number is passed securely to Twilio to bridge calls. **Your real number is never revealed to the Caller via the app interface.**
* **To Process Payments:** We use call duration and status logs to calculate the final price and process refunds or payouts on the blockchain.
* **To Improve Stability:** We use error logs (Sentry) and anonymous analytics (Vercel) to fix bugs and improve application performance.

**6. Data Sharing & Third Parties**

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with the infrastructure providers necessary to run the Service:
* **Twilio:** Processes voice calls and signaling data.
* **Vercel:** Hosts our web application.
* **Supabase:** Hosts our database.
* **Cloudflare:** Stores profile avatar data.
* **Alchemy:** To interact with the Base network.
* **Sentry:** Captures error logs for debugging.

**International Transfers:**
Our servers and third-party providers are located in the United States and the European Union. By using the Service, you acknowledge that your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States and the European Union.

**7. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)**

Regardless of your location, we grant all users the following rights:

* **Right to Access:** You can view the personal data associated with your wallet address by connecting to your profile page.
* **Right to Rectification:** You can update your name, pricing, and settings at any time via the "Edit Profile" interface.
* **Right to Deletion ("Right to be Forgotten"):** You can delete your account via the application settings.
    * **What gets deleted:** Your phone number, name, bio, and avatar are permanently removed from our active database.
    * **What we keep:** We retain your **Wallet Address** and internal **User ID** to maintain the integrity of call history and payment logs.
    * **Blockchain Warning:** We cannot delete transaction history from the Base blockchain, as it is a permanent public ledger.

**8. Data Security**

We implement industry-standard security measures, including HTTPS encryption and secure database access control. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

**9. Contact Us**

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at: **globalphone@zedcubed.com**.
